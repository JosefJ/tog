What is this thing?!


This is a community fundraiser to support a blockchain developer hub in Prague.

Also, it's a chance to get a token with real life (offline) benefits!

What will you get for your contribution?

A Token of Gratitude (ToG)! A freely tradable ERC20 token expressing my appreciation of your contribution to our community.

If you decide to redeem your token I'll invite you for a drink once you show up in Prague. And that's not all! The value of ToG grows as the streatch goals are reached.

How much money is needed?

The fundraiser doesn't have a minimal limit and each ToG holder will have a chance to get a drink. If it's raised less or more then it's needed for the cause, the leftover ether will be automatically send to charity (see the smart contract). The goals are following:

1. goal: 10 000 USD

To start and run a blockchain developer hub in Prague for one year at least.

2. goal: 25 000 USD

To organize an academic conference for blockchain related papers in 2018

Charity goal: 25 000+ USD

Everything above 25k gets automatically send directly to etherum address of Doctors without borders / Médecins Sans Frontières!

What's the token price?

ToG price is based on the amount of tokens left. Each contribution is checked for how many tokens can a contributor get and they are assigned accordingly.

Contributions below the current ToG price or above the total amount of ToG are considered to be direct donations to the contract goal.
